FAQ

Common questions.

Who is this for?
Anyone in a role with high AI automation exposure who wants to move into a more defensible position. Currently the sprint focuses on transitions from customer support into customer success, onboarding, and service operations — with more pathways coming.
What do I need to start?
A laptop, 2-4 hours per day, and your current CV. The platform provides all templates, prompt runners, and guidance. No prior AI experience required.
Is this a certification?
No. Enable produces artefacts — CV bullets, LinkedIn sections, proof packs, interview stories — not certificates. The proof is in what you can show an employer, not a badge.
Can my employer pay?
Yes. For team deployments or employer-funded access, contact us via the Bespoke tier. We also work with outplacement firms and L&D departments.
Do you guarantee employment?
No. Enable gives you the structure, artefacts, and method to execute a career transition effectively. All AI outputs must be reviewed and edited by you. The outcomes depend on your effort and market conditions.
